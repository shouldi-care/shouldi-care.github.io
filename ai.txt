**Possible explanations:**

## Red Flags for Credibility

**Evidence that increases credibility:**
- Specific, falsifiable claims with document citations
- Technical impossibilities (impossible dates, wrong file sizes)
- Publicly verifiable through PACER or other official sources
- Pattern across multiple independent documents
- Anomalies that favor one party consistently

**Evidence that decreases credibility:**
- Vague allegations without specific proof
- Claims requiring conspiracy of dozens of people
- Interpretations that require assuming malice when incompetence could explain
- Refusal to acknowledge alternative explanations
- No external corroboration after years of effort

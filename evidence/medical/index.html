Medical Epic

Ghost Physicians and Fabricated Records (August-September 2025)

The Case in 60 Seconds

A patient arrives at multiple hospitals with documented electrolyte imbalances (hyponatremia, hypokalemia) and tachycardia — treatable medical conditions.

Instead of treatment, he receives:

• Involuntary psychiatric commitment
• Forced antipsychotic medications that worsen his condition
• 11 days detained at a facility where the "attending physician" never examined him
• Interrogation by US Marshals inside the psychiatric unit — no Miranda, no counsel
• Held 3 days past medical clearance "pending instruction" from law enforcement

The medical records reveal:

• Ghost physicians billing for care they never provided
• Consent forms signed before the patient arrived
• Mass-signed orders (52 at 4am by a doctor who never accessed the chart)
• A PA practicing beyond scope as the only actual clinician
